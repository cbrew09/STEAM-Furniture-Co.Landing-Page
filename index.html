Our Story

In 2016, our founder had an idea during his Entrepreneurship class to create furniture that would inspire students to create and innovate, and that would make their environment conducive to learning. 3 years later, we're proud to say that we service 40 school districts with premium furniture designed for STEAM programs. Could your school be the next to produce a Tesla, Einstein, or Rachmaninoff? Help your students get in the mindset by supplying them with comfort and creativity all at the same time.
